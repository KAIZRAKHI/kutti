Meet the Maestro

Kalamandalam Sindhu

Kalamandalam Sindhu, one of Kerala's most celebrated Kutiyattam and Nangiarkoothu artists, has dedicated over 25 years to preserving and teaching these temple arts. Trained at Kerala Kalamandalam under legendary gurus, she has performed across India and internationally. Her mastery of rare roles and her passion for nurturing students make her a true ambassador of Kerala's classical stage tradition.
